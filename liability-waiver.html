Liability Waiver

Last updated: June 2026

My work is grounded in care, safety and meeting you where your body is. This waiver sets out the shared understanding that keeps that practice safe for everyone.

This waiver applies to any yoga, movement, breathwork, sound bath, private, online or corporate session offered by Magdarie van Staden ("Mags"). By participating in any class, session, event, retreat, workshop or recording, you acknowledge and agree to the following:

1Voluntary participation

I confirm that my participation in any yoga, movement, breathwork, sound bath or related practice — whether in person, live online, or via recording — is entirely voluntary. I understand that these practices involve physical movement and that, as with any physical activity, there is an inherent risk of injury.

2Health declaration

I confirm that I am in suitable physical and mental health to take part. Where I have any pre-existing medical condition, injury, pregnancy, or physical or mental-health limitation, I have consulted a qualified medical professional before participating, and I have disclosed anything relevant to Mags before we begin. I understand it is my responsibility to keep her informed if my circumstances change.

3Practice is not medical or psychological treatment

I understand that practising with Mags is not a substitute for medical treatment, physiotherapy, psychotherapy or psychiatric care. While the work is trauma-informed and nervous-system aware, it is not therapy or a clinical intervention. If I have a diagnosed physical or mental-health condition, I will continue to rely on care from a qualified healthcare provider.

4Assumption of risk

I understand and accept that practice may involve physical exertion, stillness, sound and breath techniques, and that there is an inherent risk of injury or discomfort. I voluntarily assume full responsibility for all risks associated with my participation, including but not limited to muscle strain, joint injury, falls, dizziness, or the aggravation of a pre-existing condition.

5Release of liability, including for negligence

To the fullest extent permitted by South African law, I release and hold harmless Magdarie van Staden, and any associated teachers, assistants, venues or staff, from any and all claims, damages, losses or expenses (including legal fees) arising from my participation in any session, event, programme or activity, whether caused by negligence or otherwise. This release does not exclude or limit any liability that cannot lawfully be excluded or limited.

6Listening to your body

I understand that it is my responsibility to listen to my body and to modify, rest in, or skip any posture, breath practice or movement that does not feel right for me on any given day. There is no posture I am required to reach. I will communicate any discomfort, pain or injury to Mags as soon as I reasonably can.

7Online & at-home practice

For online, live-streamed and recorded sessions, I acknowledge that I am practising in my own space and that it is my sole responsibility to ensure that space is safe, level, clear of hazards and appropriate for practice. Mags has no visibility of my environment and accepts no liability for any injury, loss or damage occurring in my personal practice space.

8Sound, breathwork & deep relaxation

I understand that sound baths and breathwork can evoke strong physical, emotional or energetic responses, and are not recommended for certain conditions (including, but not limited to, epilepsy, recent surgery, pregnancy or certain heart conditions) without prior medical clearance. I confirm I have disclosed any such condition, and I will rest or step away at any point if I need to.

9When a session can't go ahead

Sessions are delivered personally by Mags. If she is unable to deliver a scheduled session due to illness, personal circumstances, travel, connectivity, force majeure or any reason beyond reasonable control, the session will — at her discretion — be rescheduled, replaced with a recording, or credited. She is not liable for non-delivery in these circumstances.

10Governing law

This waiver is governed by the laws of the Republic of South Africa, and any disputes are subject to the jurisdiction of the South African courts, without prejudice to any mandatory statutory rights in your country of residence. If any provision is found unenforceable, the remaining provisions continue in full force and effect.

By participating in any class, session, event, retreat or recording offered by Magdarie van Staden, you confirm that you have read, understood and agreed to this liability waiver. Questions are always welcome — reach me at hello@magdarievanstaden.co.za.

A note from Mags. None of this changes the heart of the work. You are safe here, exactly as you are — this simply holds the container so we can both show up fully and freely.
