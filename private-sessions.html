Private Sessions

A practice shaped entirely around you.

One-to-one yoga, in person or online — met where your body actually is, not where a class assumes it should be.

This is the deepest way we can work together — a practice built around your body, your history, your nervous system and your day-to-day life.

Private sessions are trauma-informed and nervous-system aware, and grounded in ayurvedic and yogic lifestyle philosophy. Depending on what you need, we might weave together asana, pranayama, yoga nidra, functional movement, qigong and gentle lifestyle coaching. Because I take real care over who I work with and how, we begin with a short discovery call — so that by the time we practise, I already understand the person in front of me.

Step 01

Book a call

Schedule a free, no-pressure discovery call — the easiest way to start.

Step 02

A short conversation about your body, your goals, and what's drawing you here.

Step 03

Your first session

A 75–90 minute session: consultation, practice, and a debrief on what I notice in how you move and breathe — never a critique, just a place to begin.

Step 04

An ongoing rhythm

We choose a package and cadence that fits your life, with check-ins to keep it evolving.
